Organ-Aware Mixture-of-Experts Framework for Generalized Pan-Tumor Segmentation
Hancang Mi, Hong Seng Gan*, Dong Ma, Mustafa Alper Selver

We introduce OAMoE, a pioneering segmentation framework incorporating anatomical prior knowledge within a two-tier expert selection mechanism. The model categorizes input scans into pools of experts guided by anatomical knowledge, then activates the top two experts via an adaptive attention-based routing strategy. Evaluated across seven multimodal datasets spanning brain, breast, and ovary, achieving state-of-the-art performance with average Dice up to 88.5%.

DiffRefSeg: Diffusion-Guided Few-Shot Brain Tumor Segmentation via Reference Structure Fusion and Global Structural Attention
Hancang Mi, Muhammad Hanif Ramlee, Hong Seng Gan*, Wan Mahani Hafizah Wan Mahmud, Dong Ma

A coarse-to-fine few-shot segmentation pipeline integrating Reference Structure Fusion (RSF) and Global Compressed Structural Attention (GCSA) within a diffusion-based refinement backbone. Achieves Dice score of 88.31% and mIoU of 90.93% on BraTS2021, outperforming state-of-the-art methods by approximately 7%.
