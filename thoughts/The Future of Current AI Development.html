The Future of Current AI Development

AI Future

Given that AI has not yet generated sufficient social benefits, we cannot be overly optimistic about its development over the next five years. Even the most fervent investments will cool off when they fail to achieve their goals in the short term. However, this does not mean that such massive investments are a failure or meaningless. On the contrary, once we filter out the many projects that are merely riding the trend, this will build up a vast reservoir of theoretical knowledge for the future. In a sense, it also counts as supporting the future :)

In the future, achieving immortality will undoubtedly become a major societal issue. Only by introducing methods of immortality accessible to the general public can humanity avoid self-destruction. Eventually, digital immortality will complement spacecraft capable of interstellar travel.

Why bring up immortality? I believe that sooner or later, the current investments in AI by various companies will reach a dead end. When Transformers and scaling can no longer meet the demands, they will seek new directions.
